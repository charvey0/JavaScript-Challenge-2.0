Welcome to the JavaScript Challenge


Here are the rules:

  • A timer will be set to 60 seconds and begin after the countdown.
  • You will be asked questions about JavaScript (and HTML and CSS).

  • An incorrect response will reduce your time by 5 seconds.
  • When there is no time left, you will be given a final score.
  • If your score is in the top ten, you will become a member of the Hall of Fame!
